TRIAL 1
2012, 3 sets of 26 drawings
I reproduce the chapter "Im Dom" from Kafka's original manuscript of "The Trial" written in German. I printed out the images of Kafka’s manuscript which I found on the Internet, and traced the manuscript onto loose-leaf sketchbooks. There are 3 sets of 26 drawings, which are supposed to be identical but obliged to be different from one another.
