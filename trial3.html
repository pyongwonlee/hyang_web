TRIAL 3
on-going project
I transcribe the audio book of the English translation of "The Trail" written by Franz Kafka, using electronic type writer. I repeat the process to produce 8 versions of Trial 3 so far. The first Trial 3 has 37 pages and the eighth Trial 3 has 45 pages, since my typing speed becomes faster.
