WELL-TEMPERED CLAVIER
on-going project, music score books
WELL-TEMPERED CLAVIER is produced from the collection of music scores, "Well-tempered Clavier Book 1" composed and complied by Johann Sebastian Bach in 1722 and edited by Carl Czerny in 1837. "Well-Tempered Clavier Book 1" consists of 24 preludes and 24 fuges in all 24 major and minor keys in ascending order. I played "Well-Tempered Clavier Book 1" from the beginning to the end on a MIDI keyboard connected to music notation software, which instantly writes sheet music. I repeat playing "Well-Tempered Clavier Book 1" and produce 53 different versions of Well-Tempered Clavier in book form.
